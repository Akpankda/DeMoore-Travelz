Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the travel packages?

Most of our travel packages include round-trip flights, comfortable hotel accommodations (usually 3 to 5 stars), and daily breakfast. Local transportation such as airport transfers and travel between cities or attractions is typically covered. The packages also feature professionally guided tours to major landmarks and cultural sites, with entrance fees included. In addition, travelers receive 24/7 support throughout their trip to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Some packages may also include optional travel insurance, while others allow you to customize with add-ons like special dining experiences, tech-focused excursions, or adventure activities tailored to your interests.

Can I customize my tour?

Yes, we offer fully customizable itineraries to suit your preferences and travel style. Whether you want to add extra days in a particular city, focus on specific interests like technology, food, or history, or adjust your accommodation and transport options, our travel specialists will work with you to create the perfect trip. You can choose your own departure dates, select unique experiences such as private tours or local workshops, and even request specific destinations that aren't part of our standard packages. Our goal is to ensure your journey is as personalized, flexible, and memorable as possible.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance to secure the best rates on flights, accommodations, and tours—especially during peak travel seasons or holidays when demand is high. Early booking also allows more time for us to tailor your itinerary, make special arrangements, and ensure availability for exclusive experiences or limited-entry attractions. While we can accommodate last-minute bookings in many cases, planning ahead gives you greater flexibility, better options, and a smoother, stress-free travel experience.
